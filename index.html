Why Join Gitic DAO?

As a Gitic citizen, you will have the opportunity to:

  • Propose and vote on important governance issues
  • Participate in our ambassador programs
  • Benefit from our project grants

Our governance features include:

  • Smart Contract Constitution: An open, decentralized constitution written in code to ensure immutable instructions and rules for Gitic DAO.
  • States: Each blockchain network acts as a state within Gitic, fostering competition and innovation.
  • Citizenship: Every state's users are Gitic citizens, able to propose, vote, and receive incentives.
  • Yearly Budget: An open budget plan showing the distribution and allocation of funds within Gitic DAO.
