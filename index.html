Governance

Gitic DAO is (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) based on principles of transparency, inclusivity, and community-driven decision-making, where stakeholders can propose, vote on, and implement changes to the Gitic DAO's operations and policies. It is a system that enables decision-making, rule-setting, and resource allocation within the Gitic ecosystem through decentralized mechanisms. The governance framework includes the Gitic Constitution, which serves as the foundational smart contract outlining the rules and principles of the organization, as well as other components such as States (blockchain networks), citizenship, and the yearly budget.

Token Claim

The Gitic DOA Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting the development, adoption, and growth of Gitic DOA and its ecosystem. The foundation is committed to advancing the values of decentralization, transparency, and community empowerment in the blockchain space. The Gitic DOA Foundation operates independently of the core development team and is governed by a board of directors elected by the community. It is funded by donations, grants, and revenue generated from ecosystem activities, and is dedicated to serving the best interests of the Gitic DOA community.
